About Akin Engineering

Building South Sudan with integrity and innovation

Our history

Founded in Juba in 2014, Akin Engineering started as a small team of passionate architects and engineers. Over the past 12+ years, we have grown into a multi-disciplinary firm trusted by private and public clients. Our journey reflects the resilience and ambition of South Sudan itself.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional construction solutions that improve communities, while maintaining the highest standards of safety, sustainability, and client satisfaction.

Our Vision

To be the leading engineering firm in South Sudan, recognized for innovation, integrity, and transformative projects that shape the nation's future.

Core values

  • Integrity: Honest, transparent dealings with clients and partners.
  • Excellence: We pursue the highest quality in every detail.
  • Collaboration: We work as one team with our clients and communities.
  • Sustainability: Environmentally responsible practices for lasting impact.

Our approach

From initial concept to final handover, we follow a collaborative and transparent process. We listen to our clients, adapt to local conditions, and apply global best practices. Every project is managed by a dedicated team that ensures deadlines, budgets, and quality targets are met.
