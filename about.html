About Akin Engineering

Our history

Founded in Juba over a decade ago, Akin Engineering started as a small architecture studio and grew into a full‑service construction firm. We’ve built schools, hospitals, and residential complexes across South Sudan — always with integrity and precision.

Today we employ 50+ permanent staff and collaborate with international consultants to bring world‑class standards to every project.

Our Mission

To deliver sustainable, innovative infrastructure that improves communities, while maintaining the highest safety and ethical standards.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted construction partner in East Africa, recognised for quality, integrity, and transformative design.

Core values

  • Safety first – every worker goes home safe
  • Quality obsession – no shortcuts
  • Local commitment – we hire & train South Sudanese
  • Transparency – honest communication, always

Our approach

We combine architectural creativity with rigorous engineering. Every project begins with deep listening, followed by 3D modelling, material selection, and precise execution. Our integrated teams (architects, engineers, site supervisors) work under one roof to ensure seamless delivery.
